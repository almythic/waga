Founder & Nutritionist

Nadja Radonjić

Nearly two decades ago, Nadja set out to answer a question no one in nutrition was asking clearly enough: what does the body need to truly reset? The answer became WAGA — a program born in a clinical setting, refined with a team of medical specialists, and carried across four countries before finding its way to New York.

Her approach is not a diet. It is a physiological recalibration — designed to work with the body's own intelligence, not against it.

US Partner, New York

Aleksandra Balog

New York doesn't settle. Neither does Sandra. As WAGA's US partner, Aleksandra brings the same high standards that define the city's most discerning wellness culture — and the conviction that New Yorkers deserve a program built for their pace, their ambition, and their bodies.

She is the bridge between two worlds: WAGA's deep European medical tradition and the demands of the modern American market.
