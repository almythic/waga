Buttons

Four button types across three sizes. Primary (Alloy Orange) is reserved for the single most important action per screen. Secondary and tertiary for supporting actions. Botanical for nature-themed contexts.

Motion

Luxury feels unhurried. Ease-out for elements entering the viewport, ease-in for elements leaving. Organic easing is the default for most interactions. Hover any card to preview.
